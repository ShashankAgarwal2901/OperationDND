What do we do?

ZIPy! is a two wheeler rental service majorly focused on providing a new form of transportation and fun to college students of various colleges of India. This new service is available through the app and web medium , and the traditional call to book basis.

  • How are we different?

    We aim to deliver our service to colleges that require it the most , mainly those located in outskirts of cities. Our flexible packages , app-based booking , and college cultivated bike pool are sure to attract your attention

  • How it started?

    The conception of this idea came from the lack of transport we faced in and around SRM University that is both fun and economical at the same time. It is in the blood of the youth to ride their favourite bikes. ZIPy! only has one vision- "Why own a bike to ride one?"

Our product

Our bikes and scooters are equipped with GPS trackers to help our customers find them and also ensure safetly. We also provide 24/7 road assistance to those in need , whether it be directions or changing of plans. We mainly focus on gaining bookings through app and web to distinguish ourselves from our competitors by being approachable for both long and short term travel , and provide a wide variety of vehicles , both economical ones and luxurious ones.Download Our Android App to Book a Ride on the GO!

  • Prices

    Our wide variety of plans and low rates will certainly have you hooked to ZIPy! . We even provide vehicles for even an hour for a meagre 50 bucks..

ZIPy! Features

Here is what we provide you with that will keep you coming back to our services!

GPS Tracker

Our vehicles are fitted with gps trackers to help you locate them , and also for us to provide you 24/7 road assistance and directions if required.

No Deposit

We pride ourselves on taking no cash deposits from our customers. We realise the tight budget each and every college student is facing.

Offers and discounts

We like to stay active and update new offers and discounts just for you. We realize that college schedule just has those time where you just need to go out and have fun.

Vehicle preferences

Economical prices

Our prices and packages have been decided after much consideration given to all forms of transport around you.

24/7 Road assistance

We care about you and your safety on the road. We are ready for any problem you might face on the road. Feel free to contact us at any time if in need of assistance
